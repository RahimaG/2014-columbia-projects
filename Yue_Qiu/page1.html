Case Studies for CCDM Fund

Project Description and Scope

CCDMF is a national climate fund mandated to support low carbon growth and climate resilience in China. Since its inception in Nov 2007, CCDMF has engaged in bridging government and market, fiscal and financial channels, and domestic and international platforms. The Fund plays the leveraging role as seed money, mobilizing various sources into the undertaking of combating climate change. Grants and investment are two main ways to utilize its funds.

    Tasks include:
  • Collecting case studies on using PPP to finance low carbon area, focusing on:
    1. In which ways the PPP activities or models are structured? (E.g. Fund, FOF, or setting up quasi-public organization (The Green Investment Bank for example));
    2. Overall policy environment? (relevant laws or regulation to support PPP activities, and how);
    3. How does a particular fund or company or unit in the case study perform? (E.g, its financial structure, governance, business scope, major financial instrument(s), performance, etc.)
  • Provided that information is available on the case study based country, further research on how the country generally finances climate change related activities (e.g. does the country rely more on tax incentives, government spending, or market instruments, etc).
